How to Setup WiFi in Your Device

Step 1

Open the Settings app on your device.

Step 2

Go to the 'Wi-Fi' section.

Step 3

Turn on Wi-Fi if it is disabled.

Step 4

Select the desired Wi-Fi network from the available list.

Step 5

Enter the Wi-Fi password and tap 'Connect'.

Step 6

Wait for the connection to establish and ensure the Wi-Fi icon appears in the status bar.
