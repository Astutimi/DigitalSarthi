HOW TO SEARCH ON A WEB BROWSER

A web browser is a software application that allows you to access and navigate the internet. Popular browsers include Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Microsoft Edge, and Safari. Browsers interpret and display web content, enabling users to search for information, watch videos, and interact with websites.Searching on a web browser is one of the easiest ways to find information quickly. Whether you‚Äôre looking for news, recipes, tutorials, or connecting with friends on social media, search engines like Google help you discover what you need in seconds.

Let's start by following below steps to search and navigate the internet safely!


STEP 1: Open your preferred web browser (e.g., Chrome, Firefox).


STEP 2: Click on the address bar at the top.


STEP 3: Type in the search engine's URL (e.g., www.google.com) and press Enter.


STEP 4: Once the search engine loads, click on the search box.


STEP 5: Type in your query or keywords related to the information you're seeking.


STEP 6: Press Enter or click on the search button.


STEP 7: Browse through the search results to find the desired information.
