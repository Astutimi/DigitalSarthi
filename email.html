How to Send Emails on Your Smartphone

Step 1

Open your email app (Gmail, Outlook, etc.)

Step 2

Tap the 'Compose' button (usually a + icon)

Step 3

Enter recipient's email address in 'To' field

Step 4

Write your message and add attachments

Step 5

Review and tap 'Send'
